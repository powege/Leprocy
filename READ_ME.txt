### READ ME


### CODE

leprocy.R -- 
Imports leprocy case data from the World Health Organisation (WHO) and population data from the United Nations (UN). Generates two figures showing the change in leprocy cases by year: a line graph and a chorograph map.

### DATA 

population_by_country_UN_accessed_251122.csv.gz --
Human population by country downloaded from https://population.un.org/wpp/

leprocy_by_country_WHO_accessed_251122.csv.gz --
reported leprocy cases by country downloaded from https://www.who.int/data/gho/data/themes/topics/leprosy-hansens-disease

### RESULTS

cases_chorograph_map.htmp -- 
Chorograph map highlighting reported leprocy cases between 2005 and 2022. Countries with missing data are shown in grey. 

cases_line_graph.jpg -- 
Line graph showing the change in reported leprocy cases over time for different geographic regions.
